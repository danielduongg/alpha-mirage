Educational — and the opposite of advice. The point is that a strategy chosen by searching its own backtest tells you almost nothing about the future. The Deflated Sharpe Ratio (Bailey & López de Prado) prices in how many strategies you tried; below ~0.95 it's indistinguishable from luck. On a pure random walk, no edge can exist — yet the casino still hands you a Sharpe above 1.
